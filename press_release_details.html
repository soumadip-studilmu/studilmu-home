Jakarta, Indonesia 11 th April 2018- The 6th Annual BusinessGrowth Executive Learning Lounge 2018 took place in AYANA Midplaza JAKARTA on 7 th March. The program was attended by the prominent personalities such as Paulus Bambang WS, Dayu Dara Permata, Lena Setiawati, Rudy Franto Manik, Ekuslie Goestiandi and speakers from multinational companies. In the learning lounge various topics related to education, employee development, various public training classes has been discussed. The entire event showed the commitment BusinessGrowth have made in order to streamline the online education sector of Indonesia through the launch of Studilmu, Indonesia’s finest online study and certification course provider. In its effort to revitalized the education sector of Indonesia and helping this country to produce man-power that is laced with the modern education, top-notch technical training and brilliant work-efficiency, BusinessGrowth provides tailor-made online training on 40+ courses and hundreds of videos through its education wing called Studilmu.com. The CEO of BusinessGrowth and Studilmu, Berny Gomulya said “Caring for business run by clients, as we care about our own business" is fueling the spirit of BusinessGrowth service. Think and act like a business partner, not an academic advisor. "We share our clients' aspirations, work to understand their reality, and align our incentives with their objectives - so they know we're in this together.” The 6th Annual BusinessGrowth Executive Learning Lounge 2018, is the initiative of BusinessGrowth to recognize the hard work of the behind-the- scenes figures, who never tire of thinking about and planning employee development in their organizations.
